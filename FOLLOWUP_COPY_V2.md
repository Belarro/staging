# Follow-Up Copy V2 — Stage Count Change + Rewrite
**July 5, 2026. Confirmed with Ron. Supersedes the copy in FOLLOWUP_SYSTEM_SPEC.md Parts 1-2.**

## Change from V1

- **New-lead flow: stays 5 stages** (2h/2d/5d/14d/30d) — no change to count, copy rewritten.
- **Re-engage flow: cut from 5 to 4 stages** (2h/2d/5d/30d — drop the 14-day stage entirely). Reasoning: cold contacts with no prior relationship flatten hard after touch 3-4; pushing further reads as nagging, not persistence.

## New reframe (applies to both flows)

Goal is NOT "buy from us" — goal is recognition: every chef already senses two gaps and doesn't have language for them. We name them:
1. **Private grower** — nobody has a farmer who grows exactly what their kitchen asks for, seeded specifically, nothing generic.
2. **The taste that came back** — vegetables stopped tasting like they did 20-30-40 years ago; ours still do, because soil (not substrate) + same-morning harvest.
Secondary support point: **consistency** — controlled environment, same quality regardless of weather/season. Real, but a trust detail, not the hook.
